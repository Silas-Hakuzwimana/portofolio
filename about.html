About Me

Welcome to my portfolio!
I’m Silas Hakuzwimana, a passionate and innovative software developer committed to solving real-world problems through technology. My journey in the tech world began with a deep curiosity about how systems work and a desire to make an impact. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills in software development, system design, and user experience, working on a wide range of projects in various domains, including education, technology, and social impact.

Throughout my career, I’ve been fortunate enough to work on projects that align with my passion for problem-solving and creating impactful solutions. One of the most exciting projects I’ve been part of is the Water Access and Reporting System, a solution aimed at improving clean water access across Rwanda and, eventually, Africa. This system is designed to track water availability, manage issues related to water access, and provide a platform for efficient reporting and follow-ups—directly contributing to the UN's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

In addition, I’ve been actively involved in the development of ScoriTech, an AI-based student grading system that aims to automate the grading process and make education more efficient. Through my experience with AI and Machine Learning, I have developed a keen understanding of how technology can streamline everyday processes, and I strive to leverage this knowledge in all my work.

My skill set spans various programming languages and frameworks. I am proficient in:

  • Backend Development: Java, Python, Django
  • Frontend Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js(Intermediate Developer), Bootstrap, TailwindCSS (Intermediate Developer)
  • Databases: MySQL, PostgreSQL
  • AI & Machine Learning (Intermediate Developer): Developing intelligent systems for automating tasks such as grading and student assessments
  • System Design & Architecture: Building scalable and efficient systems for web and mobile applications

I take pride in my ability to think critically, solve problems, and build solutions that not only work but also enhance user experience. I am a strong advocate for clean code and best practices, ensuring that every project I work on is maintainable and scalable.

I believe that technology has the power to transform lives, especially in areas where access to resources is limited. That’s why I’m deeply invested in projects like the Water Access and Reporting System, which aims to provide sustainable solutions to pressing global challenges. My goal is to continue developing technology that makes a tangible difference, whether it’s improving education, enhancing access to clean water, or creating smarter, more efficient systems for businesses and individuals alike.

If you’re looking for a developer who is not only skilled in the latest technologies but also passionate about creating solutions that matter, I’m the right person for the job. I bring a combination of technical expertise, creative thinking, and a relentless drive to deliver results. Every project I take on is an opportunity to learn, grow, and make an impact. Whether it’s through building intuitive software, creating AI models, or designing efficient systems, my mission is to deliver quality and value that aligns with your goals.

If you’re interested in collaborating on a project or if you simply want to learn more about my work, feel free to get in touch! Let’s discuss how we can turn your ideas into reality.
